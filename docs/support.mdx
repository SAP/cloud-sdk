If you're stuck and can't find a solution to your problem, we're here to help!

## Public Support Channels

If your question or support incident do not contain internal or sensitive information use our publicly visible support channels like GitHub.com and Stack Overflow.

### GitHub.com

Please, create an issue in one of our public repositories.

- **Java**: ask a question, give feedback or create an issue [here](https://github.com/SAP/cloud-sdk/issues/new/choose).
- **JavaScript/TypeScript**: ask a question, give feedback or create an issue [here](https://github.com/SAP/cloud-sdk-js/issues/new/choose).
- **Documentation**: request documentation or suggest fixes [here](https://github.com/SAP/cloud-sdk/issues/new/choose).

### Stack Overflow

Use the `sap-cloud-sdk` tag on [Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/sap-cloud-sdk) to search for solved issues or create yours.

## For Registered SAP Customers - Use SAP One Support Launchpad

As a registered SAP customer you can use the [SAP One Support Launchpad](https://launchpad.support.sap.com/) to report issues.
We recommend using this channel when you need to share **internal or sensitive** information.
In other cases [public support channels](#public-support-channels) are preferred.

:::tip

Use **XX-S4C-SDK** component to get the incident routed directly to the SAP Cloud SDK team.

:::

## About Support

At the SAP Cloud SDK, we do our best to provide best in class support experience.
We monitor key support metrics and make sure no issues are left unanswered in our official channels.
Our official support language is English.

### Response Time

- We operate during the working hours of the Berlin CET time zone.
- We reply within **1-2 business days** to the majority of the issues.
  Our median response time is **less than 1 day**.
- The resolution time heavily depends on the issue and can't be easily predicted.

### Who Replies in Support?

Support is done directly by SAP Cloud SDK developers.
