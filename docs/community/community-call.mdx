## Community Call

In this series of calls for SAP Community you will learn about:

- The latest releases of the SAP Cloud SDK
- An In-depth focus topic around the SAP Cloud SDK
- The SAP Cloud SDK's roadmap

The call series is conducted by the SAP Cloud SDK development and product management team.

The community update calls take place every second Tuesday of a given month.
On this page, you can find the schedule as well as recordings of past sessions (see below).

[Register for the series.](https://sap-se.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_dBtYmF5IR7Gb8TMjHqEvhw)

We are working on enabling registration for individual sessions instead of a series, we will keep you posted on the progress.

### What Do You Want To Learn?

If you are interested in a particular topic, feel free to request it by [contacting us](mailto:cloudsdk@sap.com) and we'll do our best to deliver a session soon.

We consider holding a session in the future about these topics:
